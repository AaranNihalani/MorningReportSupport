1. Introduction

Welcome to The Morning Report ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by The Morning Report mobile application.

We collect the following types of data to provide our service and improve your experience:

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

4. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party partners to facilitate our services:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): For delivering advertising. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Choices

You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time via the app settings or by contacting support. You can also manage your advertising preferences through your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking on iOS).
