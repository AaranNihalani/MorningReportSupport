Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my watchlist?

Simply navigate to the "Watchlist" tab in the app. You can add stocks by symbol (e.g., AAPL) or company name, and remove them with a swipe.

Yes, The Morning Report is free to download and use. We are supported by privacy-conscious advertising that keeps our servers running.

Where does the news come from?

We aggregate data from thousands of reputable financial news outlets, regulatory filings, and market data providers via Financial Modeling Prep.
